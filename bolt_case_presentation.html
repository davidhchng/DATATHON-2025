GUIDING QUESTIONS ANALYSIS

Question 1: Revenue Strategies While Maintaining Community Focus

What We Found: Stadium operations drive 67.2% of revenue ($13.2M), merchandise shows strong growth potential (32.8%, $6.5M).

Key Insights:

  • February: Peak stadium month ($3.96M)
  • March: Peak merchandise month ($1.09M)
  • Lower Bowl most efficient revenue source
  • Jersey category dominates merchandise ($4.1M)

Recommendations:

  • Short-term: Expand seasonal pass program, optimize merchandise promotions
  • Long-term: Build digital platform, establish international programs

Question 2: Attendance Patterns & Demographics

What We Found: Average 5.7 games attended across all demographics, with 26-40 age group showing highest engagement (5.8 games).

Key Insights:

  • 18-25: Largest segment (44.8% of fanbase) - key target market
  • Seasonal pass holders: 22.4 games vs 4.5 for non-holders
  • Domestic and international fans show equal engagement
  • Consistent engagement across all age groups

Recommendations:

  • Target 18-25 demographic (largest group, 44.8%)
  • Expand seasonal pass program (5x engagement multiplier)
  • Focus on peak months for maximum revenue

Question 3: Merchandise Sales Patterns

What We Found: Jersey category dominates ($4.1M revenue), online channel 4x more effective than team store, promotions currently underperforming.

Key Insights:

  • Online: 80% of sales (4x advantage over team store)
  • Promotions: 0.56x multiplier (underperforming)
  • Peak sales in March ($1.09M)
  • Jersey commands premium pricing ($152 average)

Critical Issue:

Current promotions show 0.56x multiplier - actually reducing revenue effectiveness. This represents major optimization opportunity.

Recommendations:

  • Expand online presence (4x advantage)
  • Fix promotion strategy (currently underperforming)
  • Focus on top-performing categories
  • Develop seasonal marketing campaigns

Question 4: Matchday Experience Optimization

What We Found: Seasonal pass rate only 6.8% with massive engagement impact, 26-40 age group most engaged.

Key Insights:

  • Lower Bowl most efficient revenue source
  • Significant opportunity for experience enhancement
  • Seasonal pass holders show exceptional loyalty
  • Consistent engagement across demographics

Recommendations:

  • Expand seasonal pass program (5x engagement multiplier)
  • Enhance food & beverage options
  • Improve premium seating experience
  • Develop youth-focused matchday experiences

Question 5: Operational Constraints

What We Found: 100% international merchandise focus, online channel dominance (80.1%), promotion strategy underperforming, stadium efficiency varies significantly.

Key Constraints:

  • 100% international merchandise focus limits domestic growth
  • 80% online vs 20% team store creates channel imbalance
  • Promotion effectiveness 0.56x multiplier
  • Stadium efficiency varies 22.4x across sources

Recommendations:

  • Develop domestic merchandise strategy
  • Optimize team store experience
  • Fix promotion strategy
  • Standardize stadium operations
  • Expand international fan engagement

Question 6: Data-Driven Decision Making

What We Found: Jersey highest priced category ($152 average), promotion effectiveness 0.56x multiplier, peak sales in March, customer segmentation opportunities.

Key Insights:

  • Pricing strategy shows category variations
  • Promotion effectiveness reveals underperformance
  • Customer segmentation identifies opportunities
  • Seasonal patterns show March peak

Recommendations:

  • Implement dynamic pricing for high-value categories
  • Optimize promotion timing and targeting
  • Develop customer segment-specific strategies
  • Create seasonal marketing campaigns
  • Establish data-driven partnership criteria
